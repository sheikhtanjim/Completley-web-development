The vmin and vmax units allow you to access the size of the smaller or the larger side of the view port, according to the following rules: 100vmin=100vw or 100vh,whichever is larger 1vmax=1vw or 1vh ,whichever is larger so, in case of a potraits orentation, 100vmin is equal to 100vw, as the viewport is smaller horizontally than vertically for the same reason , 100vmax will be equal to 100vh. similarly, in case of a landscape orientation 100vmin is equal to 100vh, as the viewportis smaller vertically than horizontally. and of course,100vmax will be equal to 100vw here.
